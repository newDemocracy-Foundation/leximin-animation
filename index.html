The above graph visualises the progress of the leximin algorithm for performing stratified random selection of citizens for participation in deliberative mini-publics. The leximin algorithm was developed by Bailey Flanigan, Paul Gölz, Anupam Gupta (Carnegie Mellon University), Brett Hennig (Sortition Foundation) and Ariel D. Procaccia (Harvard University). The concept for the above visualisation was developed by Paul Gölz, and was implemented by Luke Thorburn (University of Melbourne).
